About Us

Welcome to Clothes4You, we are a company who provide clothes for the elderly and have been for a number of years. Due to the pandemic we have decided to make a online website as we know how hard it is for you guys to try and go out to buy quality clothing. Therefore, we have brought the clothes to you and tried to make a website for you guys that is easily accessible and useable.
